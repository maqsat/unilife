Example:

Partners of your structure in 3 lines have earned

1000$ BINAR, your income from each partner will be:

1 line of 1000$ * 10% = 100$

2 line of 1000$ * 10% = 100$

3 line of 1000$ * 10% = 100$

 

If you invite 5 people to the first line
